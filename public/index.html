About Us

Nam O's Fish Goi is one of the famous specialties of Da Nang, Vietnam. With its fresh flavor and perfect combination of ingredients, this dish has become a culinary symbol of Central Vietnam.

The Goi is primarily made from fresh mackerel, which is cleaned and thinly sliced. The mackerel is blended with fresh herbs, young mango leaves, and rice paper, creating a harmonious blend of the sweetness of the fish and the crispness of the vegetables. A special feature of this Goi is the dipping sauce made from fish sauce, garlic, chili, and lime, which provides a tangy, salty, and spicy flavor that is irresistible to anyone who tastes it.

Nam O's Fish Goi is not only appealing in flavor but is also beautifully presented. This dish is often served at parties, family gatherings, or among friends, bringing a sense of closeness and warmth. If you are a fan of Vietnamese cuisine, don't miss the chance to enjoy Nam O's Fish Goi – a dish rich in flavors from the sea and the diverse culinary culture of Central Vietnam.
